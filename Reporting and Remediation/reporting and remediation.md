# Reporting and Remediation

Now that you have successfully find the **flag** you can successfully upload or give it to the Managers.

### Reporting

You have to report the bug and explain **what process and which conclusions** you had made to get the flag.

This is importing to do to show you are a real ethical hacker and not an script kiddie.

Of course you should give images, and prove that the bugs you had found are very riskfull and replicable, not only causality or remotely.

---

### Proof of Concept:

You can do a Proof of concept to prove what you have done and its risks, then it's important to give possible solutions or explain possible solutions to it. 

It's important to not being explicit, riskfull or irresponsible to share it to whole Internet.

---

### Remediation

It's not only reporting but, actually in CTF's yes, but in real life, once you have found a but in an application, and important if it's yours, it's important to solve and check if it's replicable in other endpoints of your application.

Check for more weak configuration. It's important to learn remediation to get more knowledge about.
